## Description

This project is a 2D side-scrolling platformer set in a cyberpunk city at night. The player controls Jamie, a data hacker with low vision, navigating across glowing neon platforms while dodging patrolling enemies, laser tripwires, and spike traps to reach the goal at the end of the level.

The core design explores low vision as a gameplay mechanic — the world is intentionally dark and hard to read. Some zones are overexposed with harsh glare, others are near pitch-black. Jamie's echolocation ability briefly reveals nearby platforms and hazards, but requires standing still and carries a cooldown, creating tension between exploring carefully and moving quickly. The visual design is inspired by cyberpunk noir aesthetics: deep blacks, electric neons, and rain-soaked city silhouettes.

## Interaction Instructions

Press `START GAME` on the title screen using `Enter` or by clicking the button with the mouse.

- Use `Left` / `Right` arrow keys or `A` / `D` to move
- Press `Up`, `W`, or `Space` to jump
- Press jump again mid-air for a **double jump**
- Hold `F` while standing still on the ground to activate **Echolocation** — highlights nearby platforms and enemies with a cyan glow for 1.5 seconds, then enters a 3.5-second cooldown

**Visual & audio feedback to watch for:**

- A pulsing cyan ring radiates outward from Jamie when echolocation activates
- A small arc indicator below Jamie fills as the echolocation cooldown refills
- The neon progress bar at the top of the screen shows how far through the level you are
- Cyan glowing pillars mark checkpoints — walk through them to save your respawn point
- A spinning diamond pickup temporarily restores full platform visibility for 5 seconds
- A creeping darkness wall begins chasing you midway through the level — keep moving
- Red screen flash and screenshake signal when Jamie is hit
